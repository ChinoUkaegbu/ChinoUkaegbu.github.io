The 30 Minute Film Festival served as an avenue for us to film and edit short videos based on the theme “connection”. In this video, we decided to depict connection using money. Although we were separated by different buildings or different rooms or even different locations in general, we were still able to simulate passing the note around through Zoom and that ultimately showed that no matter how far away we may be at any time, we will always be connected.
